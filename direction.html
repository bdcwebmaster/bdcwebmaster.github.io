direction



Bernal Dental Care is located at 3283 Bernal Ave, Suite 108, Pleasanton, CA 94566, on the corner of Stanley Blvd and Bernal Ave, with easy access to Freeway 680 and 580.

If driving from Fwy 680, exit at Bernal Ave, drive about 10 minutes, you will find Bernal Dental Care on the left.

If driving from Fwy 580, exit at Santa Rita Rd, turn left at Valley Ave, pass Stanley Blvd, then Valley Ave becomes Bernal Ave, our dental office is located on the right.

If you have any difficulties finding us, please call (925) 249-9242, our friendly staff will assist you.
