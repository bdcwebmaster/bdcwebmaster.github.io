Our Office


Bernal Dental Care is located in the city of Pleasanton, CA, but serves patients from the entire Bay Area. Newly renovated in 2012, with high-tech equipment such as digital X-Ray, 3D Panoramic, Intro-Oral camera, Bernal Dental Care proudly provides exceptional care for children, adults, and seniors.

Our dedicated staff work together to provide you with high-quality comprehensive care.

  • 20 years of dental experience.
  • High-tech equipment: Digital X-ray, 3D Panoramic, and Intra-Oral camera.
  • Early morning, late evening, weekend appointments available upon request.
  • Walk-in and same-day appointments based on availability.
  • Most dental insurances accepted.
  • 0% interest financial support via CareCredit if qualified.
  • Minimum of 5 years free maintenance for all major treatments such as root canal, crown, veneer, bridge, implant, braces, denture.
  • English, Spanish, and Chinese Spoken.

Although we do offer same-day appointments if available, we encourage patients making appointments as early as possible. Our friendly staff will be happy to answer any questions you may have regarding scheduling and payment arrangements.

Early morning, late evening, and Saturday appointment available upon request. Just call (925) 249-9242. For medical emergency, please call 911.
