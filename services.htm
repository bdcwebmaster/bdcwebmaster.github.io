New Laser Periodontal Therapy for Gum Disease

Less Pain, Less Swelling, Less Bleeding, Faster Healing.

Laser Periodontal Therapy also known as LANAP is a laser-based technique for the treatment of periodontal disease using the DEKA Laser. Laser Periodontal Therapy is an amazing breakthrough in the treatment of periodontal disease. If you have been told that you need gum surgery or you have periodontal disease, this new technology replaces cutting the gum tissue with a scalpel and does not require stitches. This means an easier recovery with minimal bleeding, which preserves gum tissue and reduces root exposure and sensitivity.
