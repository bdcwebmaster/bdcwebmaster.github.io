Services


We  understand the need for consistent care, and  treat each patient as family. Our compassionate, professional doctors and staff are  dedicated to provide the highest level of care possible. We are dentist owned, patient driven. As a result, we focus more on providing excellent care, and less on the bottom line. We provide effective care that is responsive to the needs of the community of Pleasanton and cities surrounding it. We are not tied to any large dental corporation.

Our services include:

  •     Initial and periodic examination
  •     Ultrasonic cleaning, hygiene, and preventive dentistry   
  •     Cosmetic fillings
  •     Root Canals
  •     Extraction
  •     Deep cleaning and perio surgery
  •     Crown, veneer, and bridge
  •     Partial and full dentures
  •     Implants
  •     Braces and Invisalign

 

Although we do offer same-day appointments if available, we encourage patients making appointments as early as possible. Our friendly staff will be happy to answer any questions you may have regarding scheduling and payment arrangements.

We guarantee your privacy.
Your personal information is kept strictly confidential.
